Topics covered in this chapter include:

1. grid-template-columns:
defines the number and sizes of the columns of the grid.

2. grid-template-rows
defines the number and sizes of the rows of the grid.

3. grid-template
shorthand for defining both grid-template-columns and grid-template-rows
in one line.

4. grid-gap:
puts blank space between rows and/or columns in the grid.

5. grid-row-start and grid-row-end:
makes elements span certain rows of the grid.

6. grid-columns-start and grid-column-end:
makes elements span certain columns of the grid.

7. grid-area:
shorthand for grid-row-start, grid-column-start, grid-row-end, grid-column-end
all in one line
